Introduction

This policy describes how you may use the Rama trademark. Any use of a Rama trademark must follow this policy.

Rama is a registered word trademark of Plabayo BV with the European Union Intellectual Property Office. You can view the registration here: EUIPO record.

At this time there is no Rama logo trademark. This policy applies only to the word mark Rama.

Trademark law protects the public. Uses that confuse users about source or endorsement are not allowed. We must ensure that Rama is used in ways that do not mislead the community.

If you violate this policy or harm the goodwill of the Rama mark, we may require you to stop all use of the trademark.


General Goals

What we encourage

Use the word Rama with minimal restriction to refer to the Rama open source software and community.

What we do not allow

  • Use of Rama to refer to unrelated software or services
  • Use that implies association, endorsement, or sponsorship by the Rama project or Plabayo BV
  • Use that creates confusion about whether Rama is open source and free to use

Uses That Never Require Approval

Nominative use

You may use the word Rama to truthfully refer to the Rama software. This is allowed in both non commercial and commercial contexts when:

  • The reference is accurate and necessary to identify Rama
  • The use does not suggest endorsement or sponsorship
  • The word is not stylized to look like a logo
See examples →

Uses That Always Require Approval

Names and brands

  • Company names that include Rama
  • Product or service names that include Rama
  • Domain names or handles that imply official status

Commercial and merchandising

  • Merchandise and paid advertising that feature Rama
  • Branding that suggests an official partnership

Approval is unlikely for names or uses that could cause confusion about origin or endorsement.


How to Use the Trademark

Form

  • Use Rama as an adjective with a generic noun, for example the Rama framework
  • Do not use Rama as a verb or as a standalone noun
  • Give Rama distinctive treatment where possible, for example italics or capitalization

Attribution

When appropriate include this notice:

Rama is a registered trademark of Plabayo BV, used with permission.

Place this on a legal page, in documentation, or in about sections as needed.


Examples

Allowed without approval

  • This FOSS tool integrates with Rama
  • Guide for building proxies with the Rama framework
  • Free community meetup for Rama users

Needs prior approval

  • Rama Labs as a company name
  • Rama IDE as a commercial product name
  • rama.dev as a brand that implies official status
  • Merchandise that features Rama for sale
